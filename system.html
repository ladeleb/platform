What is the preferable Electoral System for the Lebanese citizens?

The aim of these maps is to show that the Lebanese citizens support the Proportional System.The PR systemgot the highest percentage amongst all the suggested systems.

These maps are classified according to three different categories :

  • Districts
  • Age
  • Gender
