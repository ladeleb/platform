The extent of citizens involvement in political life

The goal of these maps show the reality of engagement or lack of engagement of Lebanese citizens in political life which will affect the identification of target groups in any civil request movement at this stage

This section of maps is devided is devided into 3 main categories :

  • The extent of the involvement of citizens in political life by province
  • The extent of the involvement of citizens in political life by age category
  • The extent of the involvement of citizens in political life by gender

These maps highlight the important indicators related to the high proportion of membership in a political party baded on geographical distribution, and differences in these ratios between different age groups or gender .

مدى انخراط المواطنين بالحياة السياسية
