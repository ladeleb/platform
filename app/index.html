Lebanese Elections Data

These maps present the numbers of the registered voters as per the voters lists, the results of their votes, the power of their vote per electoral district, their sex and their confessions since 2005 until today.

These maps are classified according to four different categories:

  • Voter trends by confessions
  • Voter trends by gender
  • White and invalid ballots
  • Voter representation in the parliament
  • Registered Expatriate Lebanese Voters 2017

Election-related issues poll

These figures underline the extent of involvement in Lebanese political life, the degree of satisfaction with the performance of the Lebanese municipal councils and the nature of the electoral system favored by the Lebanese.

This section is divided into three categories:

  • The extent of citizens involvement in political life
  • Citizens satisfaction with the municipal councils
  • What is the preferable Electoral System for the Lebanese citizens?
